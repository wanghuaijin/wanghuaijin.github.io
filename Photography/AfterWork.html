After Work

These photos were taken during my time working at Yealink Co. from July 3, 2023, to July 26, 2024.

During this period, I resided in Huli Innovation Park, approximately 500 meters from our main building.

All photos in this section only have undergone slight color adjustments.

Landscape

Riding a bicycle through the bridge tunnel with the sunrise ahead.

Captured with a Nikon Z6 II, Nikkor 24-120mm lens at 54mm, f/4.0, 1/320 sec, ISO 100.
Shot in Xiamen on August 1, 2024, at 7 AM, by Wangxx.

The plane flew overhead while I was on the road.

Captured with a Nikon Z6 II, Nikkor 24-120mm lens at 65mm, f/4.0, 1/320 sec, ISO 180.
Shot in Xiamen on July 13th, 2024, at 7 PM, by Wangxx.

Xiang'an Bridge during the blue hour.

Captured with a Nikon Z6 II, Nikkor 24-120mm lens at 75mm, f/4.0, 1/250 sec, ISO 100.
Shot in Xiamen on July 13th, 2024, at 6 PM, by Wangxx.
