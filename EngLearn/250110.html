    0110-Shanghai to allow foreign-owned hospitals

    Wang HuaijinAbout 3 min

    Jan. 10, 2025

    Main Content

    Shanghai kicks off plan to allow foreign-owned hospitals[1]

      Shanghai has kicked off 启动 a plan to allow the establishment in China of wholly foreign-owned hospitals in key economic zones, biopharmaceutical areas 生物制药区 and downtown districts that are home to many expatriate residents. 外籍居民 [2]

      The commercial capital 这座商业之都，指上海 is the first city to announce steps after the central government 中央政府 said in September it it 指代 the central government would allow wholly foreign-owned hospitals in nine locations including Beijing.[3]

      The aim is to attract investment, improve business sentiment 改善商业信心 among foreign investors and reinvigorate 使...再复兴 the world's second-largest economy where growth has been sluggish. 缓慢的 [4]

      Shanghai will allow foreign-owned hospitals in key economic zones including the Lingang Special Area and Hongqiao business district, 商务区 the municipal government said in a statement on its website on Wednesday.[5]

      Through the initiative, 举措 n. the city aims to further the opening up of China's healthcare sector, enhance the diversity of healthcare services and improve the business environment. 营商环境 [6]

      Prospective 潜在的，可能的 foreign owners are required to demonstrate advanced hospital management concepts, models and service standards, as well as provide cutting edge medical technologies and equipment at 注意介词 at 的用法 an international level.[7]

      However, they are not allowed to establish hospitals specialising in certain areas, including traditional Chinese medicine or infectious diseases.[8]

      Foreign-owned hospitals must have at least 50% of their health professional and technical personnel from the Chinese mainland, China's National Health Commission said in November.[9]

    1. 上海启动允许外资医院的计划

      kick off: 启动 ↩︎

    2. 上海启动了一项计划，允许在中国主要经济区、生物制药区和许多外籍人士居住的闹市区设立外资独资医院。 ↩︎

    3. 今年9月，中国中央政府表示，将允许外资在包括北京在内的9个地区设立独资医院。这个商业之都是首个宣布相关举措的城市。 ↩︎

    4. 其目的是吸引投资，改善外国投资者的商业信心，重振增长缓慢的世界第二大经济体。 ↩︎

    5. 上海市政府周三在其网站上的一份声明中表示，上海将允许外资医院在包括临港特区和虹桥商务区在内的重点经济区设立。 ↩︎

    6. 通过这一举措，该市旨在进一步扩大中国医疗行业的开放，增强医疗服务的多样性，改善营商环境。 ↩︎

    7. 潜在的外国所有者必须展示先进的医院管理理念、模式和服务标准，并提供具有国际水平的尖端医疗技术和设备。 ↩︎

    8. 然而，它们不被允许建立某些领域的专科医院，包括中医或传染病。 ↩︎

    9. 中国国家卫生健康委员会去年11月表示，外资医院必须至少有50%的医疗专业技术人员来自中国内地。 ↩︎
