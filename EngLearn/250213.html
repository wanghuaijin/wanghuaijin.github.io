    Wang HuaijinLess than 1 minute

    Feb. 13, 2025

    Hegseth says Ukraine cannot expect return to old borders, NATO membership[1]

      U.S. Defense Secretary secretary: 秘书，部长
    Defense Secretary: 国防部长 = the Petagon Chief     Pete Hegseth said a return to Ukraine's pre-2014 pre-2014: 2014年之前 borders was unrealistic and the Trump administration does not see NATO membership for Kyiv as part of a solution to the war triggered by Russia's invasion.

      Speaking at a meeting of Ukraine's military allies at NATO headquarters in Brussels on Wednesday, Hegseth delivered the clearest and bluntest bluntest: 最直率的 public statement so far on the new U.S. administration's approach to the nearly three-year-old war.

      "We want, like you, a sovereign sovereign: 主权独立的 and prosperous Ukraine. But we must start by recognising that returning to Ukraine’s pre-2014 borders is an unrealistic objective," Hegseth told the meeting of Ukrainian officials and more than 40 allies.

      "Chasing this illusionary goal will only prolong the war and cause more suffering," he added.
